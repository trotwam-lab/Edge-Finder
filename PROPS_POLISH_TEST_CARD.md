# Props Polish Test Card

## 1. Best Props Top 10
- Do the top 10 feel like real opportunities?
- Are weak/low-book props sneaking too high?
- Are live props sensibly ranked against pregame props?

## 2. Live Status
- Can you tell LIVE / STARTING SOON / FINAL in one glance?
- Do live props feel visually urgent enough?

## 3. Visual Trust
- Any wrong headshots?
- Any missing logos that look broken instead of gracefully falling back?

## 4. Market Readability
- Any ugly raw market names?
- Any combo/specialty props that read awkwardly?

## 5. Overall Feel
- Does this feel sharper than before?
- Does it help you decide faster?
